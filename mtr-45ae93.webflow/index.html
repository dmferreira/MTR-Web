About MTR

Our company roots date back to the 1950’s as railroad and marine salvors.  Since 2002 our primary focus is providing supply chain solutions through warehousing and distribution associated with railcar freight.
‍
Since 2002 we have grown from 17,000 square feet to over 140,000 square feet and more is being built as you read.  In July of 2018 MTR purchased 15 acres of rail served property and is currently in the process of building our new facility with additional handling capabilities not currently offered in Nashville.
‍
During this growth we have learned an important lesson - “Work willingly at whatever you do, as unto the Lord, not unto man.”  Colossians 3:23
